From hand sign to keyword in four steps

The pipeline is simple and debuggable from end to end.

Step 1

Camera capture

The browser captures live video frames once the user grants camera permission.

Step 2

Landmark detection

MediaPipe Hands (via JavaScript) extracts 3D landmarks for the visible hand.

Step 3

PHP sign classifier

Your PHP endpoint receives the landmarks and returns a sign label and confidence.

Step 4

Keyword display

The UI updates a big keyword, changes orb color and can log everything in the debug panel.
